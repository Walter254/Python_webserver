Welcome to EE129

Course Topic Summary: This is an introductory course to communication networks. The topics include: Introduction to communications network architectures, layered approach of protocol hierarchy, protocols, and resource allocation techniques with emphasis on performance issues.

References:

  • "Data and Computer Communications", by W. Stallings, Prentice-Hall.
  • Other relevant articles and material.
